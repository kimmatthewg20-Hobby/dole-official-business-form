delete_sweep Data Cleanup

Automatically delete records older than 25 days based on date_created

The system automatically deletes official business records and their associated employees after 25 days from the date of creation. This helps maintain database performance and comply with data retention policies.

Automatic Cleanup:
  • Runs daily at 2:00 AM
  • Runs on server startup
  • Deletes records older than 25 days
  • Automatically deletes associated employees (CASCADE)
Default: 25 days. Use this to test with different time periods.
