INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Employees are required to accomplish an official business (OB) form prior to their participation and/or attendance to official functions such as meetings, field assignment. Approved/signed OB slips must be attached to the DTRs/bundy cards upon submission to the Human Resource Development Service (HRDS) or the Personnel Unit of the Internal Management Services Division (IMSD) of each regional office.
    Employees who attended trainings or seminars are required to submit a copy of their certificate of attendance/appearance to such in lieu of the OB form.
  2. An OB is applicable only for one (1) day regardless of the duration and/or start/end time of the business, except warranted.
  3. Failure to submit the duly approved OB forms or certificate of attendance/appearance shall be a ground for deduction from the vacation leave credits. Such deduction shall be counted as tardiness, undertime or whole day absence, whichever is applicable.
  4. Employees must ensure that the Certificate of Appearance in this form is duly signed by the agency or company where business is transacted.
